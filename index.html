About

I am specializing in machine learning, statistical modeling, and human-centered AI. My research focuses on word-level difficulty identification and text simplification for bilingual readers with dyslexia, integrating behavioral data, linguistic features, and large language models. I also have experience in retail recommender systems and healthcare decision support systems.
